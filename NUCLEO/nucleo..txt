ADC read - parallel interface
 1. Pulse CONVST → ADC starts converting.
   -CONVST LOW ≥ 25 ns
   -CONVST HIGH  ≥ 25 ns
 2. BUSY goes HIGH during conversion.
   -From CONVST rising → BUSY high: max 45 ns.
 3. Wait until BUSY goes LOW → data is ready.
   -Conversion time AD7606: typ 4 µs - max 4 µs.
   -If Oversampling is enabled: typical * oversampling_ratio
 4. Keep RD HIGH while BUSY is HIGH (don’t read yet).
 5. After BUSY goes LOW: Pull CS LOW & Pull RD LOW
   -BUSY falling → CS falling: min 0 ns (t4)
   -CS → RD setup: min 0 ns (t8)
   -CS → RD hold: min 0 ns (t9)
 6. Latch/read DB[15:0] on the MCU.
   -After RD goes LOW, wait the “data access” time:
      VDRIVE > 4.75 V: 16 ns
      *VDRIVE > 3.3 V: 21 ns <-- that's us*
      VDRIVE > 2.7 V: 25 ns
      VDRIVE > 2.3 V: 32 ns
 7. Bring RD HIGH (this advances to the next channel word) and do it for each channel.
    -RD low pulse width t10 ≥ 21ns
    -RD high pulse width t11: ≥15 ns
    -Data hold after RD falling t15: ≥6 ns
 8. Finally bring CS HIGH when done.
    -CS rising → DB[15:0] goes tri-state t17: ≥22 ns
